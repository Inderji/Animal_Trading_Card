Black Panther

Apart from color the black panther is believed to be less fertile than normal-colored big cats and also much more unpredictable and aggressive.

  • Scientific Name: Panthera onca
  • Average Weight: 29 to 91 kg
  • Lifespan: 12-15 years
  • Habitat: Rainforest, marshland, woodlands, and even mountains.

Usually panthers are also called leopards or big cats. The name panther has been eliminated today but still the pure black leopards are called panthers. Black panthers are really beautiful but rare. They are usually found in the thick forests of united states, UK and Australia.
